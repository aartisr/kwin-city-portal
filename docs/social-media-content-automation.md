# KWIN City Social Media Content And Automation Strategy

This guide turns the KWIN City Portal into a repeatable Instagram and Facebook publishing system.

The goal is not fake virality, engagement pods, or spam. The goal is a credible content engine that people save, share, and discuss because it explains KWIN City clearly, visually, and with source discipline.

Current public channels:

```text
Instagram: https://www.instagram.com/hellokwincityconnect
Facebook: https://www.facebook.com/kwincity
Website hub: https://kwin-city.com/instagram
```

## 1. Operating Principle

Every post should do one of three things:

1. Explain what KWIN City is.
2. Show why North Bengaluru matters.
3. Prove that the portal handles claims responsibly.

Use this evidence label in every caption or visual where a factual claim is central:

```text
Verified: backed by official or primary records.
Context: useful regional evidence, but not proof of a KWIN-specific commitment.
Pending: present in project material, but awaiting stronger public verification.
```

Avoid these:

```text
Guaranteed jobs
Guaranteed investment returns
Official endorsement language unless the source proves it
Unverified tenant, university, or partner claims
```

## 5. The Viral Content Loop

Use this structure for every high-effort post.

```text
Hook: one plain-English question or surprising framing.
Context: explain why the fact matters.
Evidence: show the source status.
Visual: make it easy to save or share.
Action: ask one specific response.
Link: send people to the bio link or Facebook clickable link.
Follow-up: turn good comments into the next post.
```

Good hooks:

```text
What is KWIN City in 30 seconds?
Why is North Bengaluru getting this much attention?
Which KWIN City claims are verified, and which are still pending?
What does a 465-acre knowledge district actually mean?
Why do airport corridors shape new city projects?
```

Good calls to action:

```text
Save this if you are tracking North Bengaluru.
Share this with someone comparing Bengaluru growth corridors.
Comment "sources" if you want the evidence ledger next.
Open the brief through the link in bio.
On Facebook: read the source-linked brief here: https://kwin-city.com/instagram
```

## 8. Automation Strategy

Use three phases. Do not jump straight to direct API auto-posting until the business accounts, permissions, and content review process are mature.

### Phase 1: Semi-Automated Publishing

Best for immediate launch.

Steps:

1. Build 30 posts from the calendar above.
2. Create carousel/reel/static visuals in Canva, Figma, or another brand template tool.
3. Save every caption in a calendar spreadsheet.
4. Schedule Instagram and Facebook posts in Meta Business Suite.
5. Check analytics every morning.
6. Rewrite the next week based on saves, shares, comments, and link taps.

Benefits:

```text
Fastest to start
Lowest technical risk
Human review before every post
No API approval blocker
```

### Phase 2: Portal-Generated Content Queue

Best after the first 30 days.

Build a small internal queue that creates draft posts from portal content.

Recommended files:

```text
app/data/social/topics.ts
app/data/social/templates.ts
scripts/generate-social-calendar.mjs
docs/social-calendar/YYYY-MM.md
```

The generator should produce drafts, not publish directly:

```text
date
platform
format
topic
hook
caption
visual brief
source URL
evidence status
UTM link
approval status
```

Daily workflow:

1. A scheduled job creates tomorrow's draft.
2. A human reviews the evidence label and caption.
3. Approved posts are scheduled in Meta Business Suite or a publishing tool.
4. Performance notes go back into the next queue.

### Phase 3: Direct API Publishing

Best only after account setup and approvals are complete.

Use Meta's official APIs:

```text
Instagram Content Publishing API:
https://developers.facebook.com/docs/instagram-api/guides/content-publishing/

Facebook Page publishing:
https://developers.facebook.com/docs/pages-api/posts/
```

Typical requirements to confirm in the Meta developer dashboard:

```text
Instagram professional account
Instagram account connected to the Facebook Page
Meta app
Page access token
Instagram business account ID
Permissions such as instagram_basic, instagram_content_publish, pages_show_list, pages_read_engagement, and pages_manage_posts
App review for production use
```

Important API realities:

```text
Instagram publishing usually needs a public image_url or video_url that Meta can fetch.
Instagram is not a plain text publishing surface.
Facebook Page posts can carry clickable links more naturally.
Never store access tokens in source control.
Use environment variables in Vercel or the deployment platform.
Keep a human approval gate unless the post type is fully templated and low risk.
```

Recommended environment variables:

```text
META_APP_ID
META_APP_SECRET
META_PAGE_ID
META_PAGE_ACCESS_TOKEN
INSTAGRAM_BUSINESS_ACCOUNT_ID
SOCIAL_PUBLISHING_ENABLED=false
```

Vercel-friendly daily job shape:

```text
Vercel Cron runs once per day.
The job reads approved posts from the queue.
The job publishes only posts where approvalStatus is "approved".
The job records the returned platform post ID.
The job never republishes an item with a platform post ID.
```

## 9. Daily Checklist

Do this every morning:

```text
Check yesterday's saves, shares, comments, link taps, reach, and follower growth.
Reply to credible comments.
Capture the best question as a future post.
Verify today's caption has the right evidence label.
Confirm the link points to the correct portal page.
Publish or confirm the scheduled post.
Add one story that points to the day's post.
```

Do this every Friday:

```text
Find the top 3 posts by saves and shares.
Find the top 3 questions in comments or DMs.
Turn one high-performing post into a reel.
Turn one high-performing comment into an FAQ post.
Refresh next week's calendar.
```

## 12. When To Automate Publishing Directly

Direct publishing makes sense only when all of these are true:

```text
The Instagram account is professional.
The Instagram account is connected to the Facebook Page.
The Meta app has the required permissions approved.
Tokens are stored securely.
The post queue has human approval status.
There is a rollback process for incorrect posts.
The content format is predictable.
```

Until then, automate the calendar and drafts, but publish through Meta Business Suite.
