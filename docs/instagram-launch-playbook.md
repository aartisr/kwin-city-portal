# KWIN City Instagram Launch Playbook

This playbook turns the KWIN City portal into an Instagram-ready social presence without weakening the evidence-first posture of the site.

The account itself must be created by a human owner because Instagram requires direct control of the email or phone, password, login approvals, and any identity checks. Once the account exists, use this plan to make it polished from day one.

## What Has Been Prepared in the Website

Use this URL as the Instagram link in bio:

```text

```

- the KWIN City brief

## Account Setup

### 1. Create the Account

Create the account using an owned brand email address, not a personal email.

Recommended email:

```text
hello@kwin-city.com
```

Recommended username order:

```text
kwincity
kwin.city
kwincityofficial
kwincity.in
kwin.city.in
```

Recommended display name:

```text
KWIN City
```

Do not use "official" unless the operator is comfortable representing the account as the official brand channel.

### 2. Switch to a Professional Account

Use a Professional account, preferably Business.

Instagram's official Help Center describes Professional accounts as either Business or Creator accounts. Business is the better fit for organizations looking to grow and reach customers, and it unlocks features such as a professional dashboard, contact information, and insights.

Suggested category:

```text
Information Service
```

Alternatives:

```text
Community Organization
Urban Planning
Education
News and Media Website
```

Choose the category that is actually available in Instagram and best matches the account's legal/public posture.

### 3. Secure the Account

Before posting:

1. Turn on two-factor authentication.
2. Add at least two trusted admins in Meta Business Suite.
3. Save backup codes in a secure password manager.
4. Use a unique password.
5. Do not share login credentials in chat, email, or public docs.
6. Connect only approved scheduling tools.

## Visual Direction

The feed should feel like:

```text
evidence desk + city brief + future-facing public explainer
```

Use:

- deep navy backgrounds
- gold for KWIN identity
- cyan for data and infrastructure
- emerald for wellbeing and sustainability
- white space
- maps, diagrams, numbers, and short labels
- source badges

Avoid:

- fake render-heavy hype
- generic smart-city stock images
- screenshots too dense to read
- exaggerated claims
- follower-buying or engagement pods
- unverified "official" claims

## Comment and DM Policy

Use this moderation posture:

```text
Warm, clear, evidence-led.
```

Reply style:

```text
Thanks for asking. The current source-linked page is here: <link>. If you have a better public source, send it and we will review it.
```

Do not debate unsupported claims in comments. Move detailed verification to the source pages and trust page.

Escalate:

- legal threats
- identity or account impersonation
- hate speech
- spam
- requests for investment advice
- private land, acquisition, or compensation questions

## Accessibility Rules

Every post should include:

- readable contrast
- large text
- alt text
- captions on reels
- no tiny footnotes
- no flashing animation
- clear source URL in caption

Alt text example:

```text
KWIN City carousel cover showing the words Knowledge, Wellbeing, and Innovation over a dark navy background with gold, cyan, and emerald accents.
```

## Measurement

Track weekly:

- profile visits
- website taps
- saves
- shares
- carousel completion
- reel watch time
- comments with real questions
- DMs that request sources or collaboration

Best early signal:

```text
saves + shares + source-page clicks
```

Do not optimize only for likes.

## Compliance Guardrails

Follow the site's webmaster guidelines:

- do not buy followers
- do not buy links
- do not use engagement pods
- do not mass-comment from automation tools
- do not post claims that cannot be traced to a source or clearly labeled as pending verification
- do not imply government endorsement unless officially documented

The goal is not artificial popularity. The goal is durable trust.
